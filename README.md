### **Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette** (Module 4)
Demonstrated professional digital communication skills through two channels:

**Part A - Email Drafts:**
1. Student → Professor: Request for assignment extension (following all professional protocols)
2. Student → Internship Coordinator: Expression of interest in summer internship

Both emails include proper subject lines, professional greetings, well-structured bodies, and appropriate sign-offs.

**Part B - Social Media Guidelines:**
Created a comprehensive checklist with 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student, covering privacy, professional branding, verification, and platform-specific guidelines.

**Case Study:** Analyzed a real-world incident where poor email etiquette cost a student an internship opportunity, demonstrating the tangible consequences of unprofessional digital communication.

**Deliverable:** 2 professional emails + social media checklist + scenario analysis

---

### **Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention** (Module 5)
Researched and documented cybercrime awareness resources to help peers stay safe online:

**Part A - Case Study:**
- **Crime Type:** UPI/Online Payment Fraud (specifically the "Wrong Transfer" scam)
- **Content:** 250-word detailed analysis covering:
  - What the crime is and how it operates step-by-step
  - Who is typically targeted (college students aged 18-30)
  - Real-world consequences and statistics (2.5 lakh cases in 2023, 300% increase since 2020)
- **Source:** National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)

**Part B - Prevention Checklist:**
Created a comprehensive "Stay Safe Online" guide with 12 specific, actionable tips including:
- General online safety (passwords, 2FA, phishing prevention, software updates)
- UPI/Financial safety specific to Indian students
- Emergency response procedures
- Reporting channels: **National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) | Helpline: 1930**

**Personal Reflection:** Documented surprising insights from research and committed to implementing 2FA on all accounts + weekly transaction monitoring.

**Deliverable:** Case study + 12-point prevention checklist
